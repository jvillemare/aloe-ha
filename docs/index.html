A New Garden Designer

A free, open-source JavaFX application.

Now with over 4,000 North American plants

Download

Goes to the GitHub releases page.

Plot Designer

A free form, drag-and-drop interface allows you to drag in one of many selected or existing plants into the center plot. Several algorithms constantly evaluate the number of animals fed, continous bloom, plants that match your questionnaire criteria, and the degree to which plants are next to similarly tall plants.

You can save and reload your garden plot easily as a .gardenproject file anywhere on your disk.

Future Previewer

You can view your garden as it will look in a few years in advance, as well as the season of the year, too.
